## February
![Hyd](https://res.cloudinary.com/daoccbmae/image/upload/v1775477455/96E2E9ED-F21B-4DB5-AF2D-FE0423532D71_1_105_c_d3yu77.jpg)

February was very normal. On the 8th of February which was Sunday, we went to explore **Ibrahimpatnam** area. It's far from the city like 40 km or so. Later in the same evening we had a comedy show for **Vir Das**. I would say the show was one of the good shows. I really liked how he did overall for everything. He kept us hooked till end with the storytelling and everything.

Went to **Numaish Exhibition** which happens every year in Hyderabad. I would say it's kind of overrated, a lot of crowd like a lot. Not worth it but if someone goes early then maybe it's good.

![Hyd](https://res.cloudinary.com/daoccbmae/image/upload/v1775100837/92615E34-E13A-49F9-A556-1E11F7E0F8E4_1_105_c_kqawqj.jpg)

I explored **Forestrek Park** which is just few km away from the city. I did some running, explored the whole place. A park with treks and walkways through beautiful wooded areas and small rocky hills. It's located near Narsingi.

Not much happened in February.
